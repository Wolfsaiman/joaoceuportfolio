Photoshop Edits

This is my Photoshop projects' page. Photoshop is that one kind of digital art that I do the most. I have a lot of Photoshop edits but I decided to gather the best ones in this page to show you.

Remember that every edit in this page was made for fun and not a professional company or something. The projects shown here are silly projects that came to my mind one day or another.

Springle

This is Springle, a mix of 2 characters from the game "Five Nights at Freddy's", Springtrap and Mangle. Since they're two of my favorite characters in game, I decided to make a fusion with those two. Springtrap is my favorite character, so I used him for base model and added some of Mangle's parts (ironic, since Mangle in game is demountable), creating Springle.

Dr. Nemesis

This is another mix of two game characters. Dr. Nemesis is the mix of The Doctor and The Nemesis, 2 characters from the game "Dead By Daylight". Nemesis is originally from the game "Resident Evil" but Dead By Daylight usually adds characters from other games, and Nemesis was one of them. I decided to use Nemesis as the base model and then added The Doctor's head and used his weapon as Nemesis' tentacle. I also painted Nemesis' clothing to make it more similiar to The Doctor.

Fusion

Yes, most of my projects (at least the best ones) are mix of characters. This time it's a mix of 11 characters from Mortal Kombat 11 (actually it was a big coincidence that I accidentally used 11 characters for the 11th game). Fusion is the mix of Kung lao, Kano, Sheeva, Erron Black, Kollector, Baraka, Jax, Kabal, Raiden, Scorpion and Sub-zero. I decided to use the main parts of those characters, making Fusion even more powerful, for example, I used Kung Lao's hat because it's his special ability.
