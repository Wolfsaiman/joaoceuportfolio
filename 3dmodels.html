3D Modeling

I started 3D modeling not long ago, so I'm still a beginner. I can do some alien busts, customize pre-made models, etc... I use Blender for 3D modeling, it's the best software for 3D modeling in my opinion.

The model shown in this page is a pre-made model that I fully changed, so you wouldn't recognize the original model if you haven't seen it.

Saiman

This is Saiman, my OC (Original Character) who never had an official appearance until I made this model. I created him with some ideas and my friend drew it on a scratch paper. After that, people have been creating some fan arts of him, but nothing was really official. Now with this model you can see how he actually looks.

Saiman was supposed to have a scar on his eye, however, due to some problems on texturing him I couldn't make the scar in one eye only. He was also supposed to have some other scars on his body, but the same problem happened so this model is a "clean" version of Saiman

Saiman is very tall, he is 195m. He also have a huge fluffy tail and a soft and short fur. Saiman also has thick thighs because he is a wolf.

After making the model, I decided to do a short animation to test his movements. I also like to do 3D animation, even though I didn't really do anything so far. I did some tests like characters walking and waving, and this one is Saiman waving in a gif.
